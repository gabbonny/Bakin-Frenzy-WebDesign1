The Process

These are my usual steps of learning a new recipe  and trying out new tools!!

Learn

  • Time to research recipes
  • FB, Pinterest, TV, other social media for inspiration!
  • Watch some videos and gather your tools!

Bake

  • Get ready to work your muscles
  • Combine your ingredients, measure correctly
  • Patient is a virtue, bake to perfection!

Record

  • Record your success!
  • Take notes for improvements
  • Take pics of your creation!
